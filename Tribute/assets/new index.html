William (WALI) Albert Martin was born on September 29, 1949 to the union of William Sr. and Cordelia in Chicago. William received his formal education at Farren Grammar school and Dusable High School in Chicago, Illinois, He furthered his education at Roosevelt University. William accepted Mohammed as his Savior in 1970 at the age of 21. He was united in holy matrimony with Marneeta M. Hagood on December 18, 2004 in Waukegan, Illinois. William enlisted into the United States Army and served for eight years. He was also a Gulf War Veteran. Through out his life, William was employed by the Chicago Fire Department; was a building manager, and also became an insurance salesman. In his leisure, he enjoyed painting, drawing, fishing, and games. He was a member of the Friendship Club and the 12 STEP Club
